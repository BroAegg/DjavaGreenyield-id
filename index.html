About Us

DJAVA GREENYIELD INTERNATIONAL is proud to be part of Indonesia’s fast-growing charcoal briquette industry. We bring the best of Indonesian charcoal to the world—carefully processed, high-quality, and ready for global markets. Our briquettes are already trusted in Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and America. With consistent quality and customer satisfaction as Our top priorities, we’re here to deliver charcoal briquettes you can rely on—every time, everywhere.
